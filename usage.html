GirGit - How to Use & Workflow

Getting Started

GirGit is designed to be a privacy-first, keyboard-driven AI assistant that stays local to your system. Here’s how to begin using it effectively:

  • Start the application and select your preferred AI provider (ChatGPT or Gemini).
  • Use global keyboard shortcuts to control the app without interrupting your workflow.
  • Customize prompts if needed to tailor AI responses to your needs.

Typical Workflow

  1. Choose Provider: Use the launcher window to select ChatGPT, Gemini, or custom prompt mode.
  2. Capture Screenshot: Press Ctrl + L to take a screenshot of any area on your screen.
  3. OCR & Process: GirGit performs local OCR on the captured screenshot to extract text.
  4. Send Prompt: Use Ctrl + Enter to send the extracted text with your custom prompt to the AI provider.
  5. View Response: The AI response will appear in the preview or main window, depending on your configuration.
  6. Audio Interaction: Optionally, toggle audio recording and transcription with Ctrl + Space.
  7. Mouse Interaction: By default, the app is keyboard-driven. Enable mouse interaction temporarily with Ctrl + Shift + M.
  8. Window Movement & Control: Use arrow keys with Ctrl to move app windows around your screen for convenience.

Keyboard Shortcuts Summary

  • Ctrl + ↑↓←→ — Move windows
  • Ctrl + Shift + ↑↓ — Scroll page up/down
  • Ctrl + / — Toggle main window visibility
  • Ctrl + L — Capture screenshot
  • Ctrl + Enter — Send prompt to AI
  • Ctrl + Space — Toggle audio recording
  • Ctrl + Shift + M — Toggle mouse interaction
  • Ctrl + D — Delete all screenshots
  • Ctrl + Backspace — Delete last screenshot
  • Ctrl + Q — Quit app

Privacy & Security

All data processing is done locally on your machine.
GirGit does not store, send, or proxy any personal data.
AI interactions happen directly between you and the AI provider using your own credentials.
