About

GirGit runs entirely on your system, creating a bridge to AI providers like ChatGPT and Gemini using your own accounts. It acts as a local interface layer, ensuring that no data is ever proxied, stored, or transmitted to us.

Why GirGit?

  • Local-FirstDesigned to run on your machine, not on a remote server.
  • Zero TelemetryNo analytics, no tracking, strict privacy.
  • Keyboard CentricGlobal shortcuts for focused, mouse-free workflows.
  • Stealth ModeBuilt to stay out of your way until you need it.

Key Features

  • Global ShortcutsSummon AI instantly from any application.
  • BYO AccountSecure login to ChatGPT / Gemini via local webview.
  • Visual IntelligenceLocal screenshot capture + OCR workflow.
  • Custom PromptsDefine your own system prompts for specialized tasks.
  • Multi-WindowMini, Preview, and Main windows for multitasking.

Privacy Architecture

GirGit acts only as a local interface layer. All interactions happen directly between your machine and the AI provider (OpenAI/Google). We cannot see your chats, your screenshots, or your history.

Interaction Modes

  • Keyboard Mode (Default) Fully shortcut-driven for speed and efficiency.
  • Interactive Mode Enables mouse interaction when explicitly toggled.
